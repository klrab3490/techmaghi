India Wins ICC T20 World Cup 2025 in Nail-Biting Final

By Sports Desk | June 16, 2025

India clinched a thrilling victory against Australia in the ICC T20 World Cup final at Lords, London. The match came down to the last over, with India chasing 189. A six by Rinku Singh sealed the win, sparking celebrations across the country.

Captain Hardik Pandya credited the win to the team’s unity and preparation. “This was not just a match, it was a message,” he said during the post-match press conference. Prime Minister Narendra Modi congratulated the team, calling it a “moment of pride for every Indian.”

AI Revolution: How Artificial Intelligence Is Reshaping Jobs in 2025

By Rahul A B | June 18, 2025

As AI systems become increasingly integrated into workplaces, companies are rapidly adopting automation for tasks ranging from customer service to logistics. According to a recent report by the World Tech Forum, over 30% of clerical jobs in the US and India have been automated since 2023.

Experts believe that while some jobs may disappear, new roles in AI monitoring, ethics, and creative collaboration are being born. “We’re not facing a job crisis but a transformation,” said Anjali Menon, an AI policy analyst. Educational institutions are also restructuring their courses to include AI-focused curricula from the high school level.

Monsoon 2025 Hits South India Early, Heavy Rainfall Predicted

By Staff Reporter | June 17, 2025

The Indian Meteorological Department (IMD) has announced that the southwest monsoon has arrived earlier than expected across Kerala and Tamil Nadu. Torrential rains have already disrupted train services and flights in Kochi, Trivandrum, and Coimbatore.

Farmers in the region have welcomed the early showers, hoping for a strong agricultural season. However, authorities have warned of potential flooding in low-lying areas. Citizens are advised to avoid unnecessary travel and to keep emergency kits ready. Relief teams are on standby in 9 districts, IMD confirmed.
